Science

Science

Vitamins & minerals power your cells. Without them, no power, no life.

The more energy you use, the more vitamin and mineral you need. They activate the most critical enzyme reactions in the body.

Without this activity, life would cease to exist. They ensure the proper functioning of digestion, hormones, acid/alkaline balance, nerve conduction, muscle activity, detoxification, and cell synthesis & repair.

Science

How are activated vitamins and minerals formed?

The Sun generates the vitamins in plants and animals we eat. Green plants transform sunlight into energized vitamins & minerals that are transferred to anything eating those plants.

Science

So why do most people today need vitamin and mineral supplements to rebalance their metabolism for full health?

  • Synthetic fertilizers deplete soils of minerals, so any plants raised in those soils (or animals raised on those plants) are missing most vitamins and minerals. This can make the foods you eat incomplete, robbing you of energy and health.
  • Toxins deplete your body of vital vitamins and minerals required for detoxification and tissue repair. Toxic metals and chemicals are found in food, water, air and in everyday products like cosmetics and household cleaners.
  • Stress – even good stress, like exercise, puts strains on our mineral and nutrient reserves.
  • Dietary Choices, improper supplementation & other substances like alcohol, pharmaceuticals & other drugs strain & disrupt nutrient balance.

Why use hair to measure minerals?

    Metabolic Activity in Cells
  • Detects Hidden

  • Detects Hidden
    Toxic Exposures
  • Stable, Long

  • Painless &

Blood tests are subject to fluctuations based on your activities, emotions, and food you ate most recently. A hair test, when performed and analyzed properly, provides a long-term record of nutrient and toxin intakes and of potential levels of minerals stored in your body.
