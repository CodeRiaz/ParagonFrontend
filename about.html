About

What we stand for

Paragon® is a new type of health company. We are passionate about what we do. We stand for authenticity, honesty, simplicity & common sense. We fully understand the potential of each unique human body to naturally heal & thrive given the right nutritional support.

We are propelled by achieving real results. For over 30 years we have been pioneers in the field of integrative medical research. Our rigorous case studies used clinical testing to help redefine how toxins and other stressor deplete our bodies of vitamins and other nutrients. That (Our) independent & relentless pursuit of excellence in scientific knowledge & understanding, coupled with our world-class clinical testing partnerships, have led to the development of our 10+ year-proven proprietary algorithm and unmatched insights in product R&D.

We combine superior & unique multi-input analysis with unrivalled product formulations that tap the very best ingredients, creating the ideal blueprint for rebalancing biochemistry, thereby dramatically increasing each cell’s efficiency & thereby optimizing wellness. Together they generate astonishing and traceable clinical results.

Now, equipped with an unprecedented understanding of the factors driving real human health, and with an intense desire to deliver effective, effortless solutions at a fair price, our purpose is singular: To seriously make your life better.
